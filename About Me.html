Who am I?

Hi, my name is David Walker, a twenty years old young boy living in Kingston, St.Andrew, Jamaica.
Currently, I am attending The University Of Technology at the Papine Campus where I study to become a Computer Programmer and Technician.
Growing up, there was a time I had been curious to know what the inside of a Computer Tower was like.
We have all used it to connect USB chord, flash drives, thumb drives, inserted Compacted Disks into CD Driver and so on.
These things intrigued me to find out what was beneath the surface of what we have been using for so long.
Not just pulling the various peripherals apart but also putting back together sparked my aspirition.

The sports of basketball holds a close place in my heart, as it is a sport I had never known I would like to play.
Once I got the oppotunity to partake in the sport, I discovered that I could shoot very well without any training.
Though there was a long time before I joined a professional school team, I had soon seen how great discipline was to become better at
playing the sport and playing better to help a team win was was something I liked.
